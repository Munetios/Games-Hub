Quick Info

In normal mode, click a piece, then click a destination square. In editor mode, choose a piece from the palette and click any square to place it. Use the eraser to remove pieces.

This version includes check, checkmate, stalemate, promotion, captured pieces, board flipping, AI, presets, and custom starting positions.
