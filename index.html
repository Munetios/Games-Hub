🐍 Snake

Controls: Arrow Keys / WASD, Space to Pause

Objective: Eat food to grow longer without hitting walls or yourself

Tips: Use custom maps, avoid corners when long, plan your route ahead

Features: Custom map editor, multiple difficulty levels, power-ups

👻 Pac-Man

Controls: Arrow Keys to move

Objective: Eat all dots while avoiding ghosts. Eat power pellets to chase ghosts

Tips: Plan escape routes, use corners to lose ghosts, save power pellets for emergencies

Features: Classic ghost AI, power mode, multiple levels

♟️ Chess

Controls: Click to select and move pieces

Objective: Checkmate opponent's king

Tips: Control the center, develop pieces early, protect your king

Features: Player vs AI mode, legal move validation, check detection

🧱 Tetris

Controls: ← → to move, ↑ to rotate, ↓ to drop faster

Objective: Complete horizontal lines to clear them and score points

Tips: Leave space for I-pieces, avoid creating holes, plan ahead

Features: Classic gameplay, ghost piece preview, increasing difficulty

♠️ Solitaire

Controls: Click to select/move cards

Objective: Move all cards to foundations (Ace to King by suit)

Tips: Expose face-down cards first, build foundations evenly, plan moves carefully

Features: Full Klondike rules, move counter, timer, win detection

🔢 2048

Controls: Arrow Keys or Swipe

Objective: Combine tiles to reach 2048

Tips: Keep highest tile in corner, build in one direction, plan several moves ahead

Features: Undo feature, score tracking, smooth animations

💣 Minesweeper

Controls: Left-click to reveal, Right-click to flag

Objective: Reveal all safe cells without clicking mines

Tips: Numbers show adjacent mines, flag suspicious cells, start with corners

Features: Multiple difficulty levels, timer, mine counter

🔴 Checkers

Controls: Click to select and move pieces

Objective: Capture all opponent pieces or block all moves

Tips: Kings are powerful, control center, force trades when ahead

Features: AI opponent, forced jumps, king promotion
